## Project Step 
#### USE CASE: display content
##### Actors
teachers

##### Trigger
Teacher indicates they want to know student grades

##### Precondition
Student grades have not populated 

##### Post-condition
Users grades will be posted on screen

##### Normal Flow
1. The Teacher selects a course 
2. The Teacher selects the grade menu 
3. The Teacher clicks on course grade
4. The Teacher grade is displayed  

##### Alternate Flow
A. The Teacher will find that the grades have not been populated. 
1. The Teacher creates a course grade file
2. The Teacher will add students 
3. The Teacher will save
