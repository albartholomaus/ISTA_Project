## Project Step 
#### USE CASE: Grade Display
##### Actors
Student

##### Trigger
customer indicates they want to know there grades

##### Precondition
Student grades have not populated 

##### Post-condition
Users grade will be posted on screen

##### Normal Flow
1. The student selects a course 
2. The student selects the grade menu 
3. The student clicks on course grade
4. The students grade is displayed  

##### Alternate Flow
A. The student will find that the grades have not been populated. 
1. The student selects a course 
2. The student selects the grade menu 
3. The student is Prompted that the course has not stated. 
4. The student is returned to Step 2.
